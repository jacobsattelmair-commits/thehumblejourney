Bio

I'm Jacob Sattelmair — a founder and builder working at the intersection of aging, health, and technology.

Based in Boston, I'm currently building Alder, a company focused on helping older adults live well at home. Before this, I spent years working across public health, data science, and health services — translating research into programs and products that reach people at scale.

This site is a place for me to think out loud — about the work, the ideas, and the journey in between.
