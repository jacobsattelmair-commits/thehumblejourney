About

Jacob Sattelmair is passionate about using business and technology innovation to scale services that improve peoples' health and quality of life.

He is the co-founder and CEO of Alder, a business that helps families with older relatives navigate the complexities of aging. He previously founded and led Wellframe, a digital health company that modernizes nurse-led care management for patients living with clinical complexity.

Jacob holds a Doctor of Science in Epidemiology from the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health, a Master of Science in Physiology from Oxford University, and a BA in Neurobiology from Harvard University.

He lives in Boston with his wife and 2 children, and loves to move in nature.
